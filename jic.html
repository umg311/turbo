Our Journey

Our Mission

From the moment we were chosen to represent ISC-DIP at the prestigious UAE National Electric Vehicle Grand Prix (EVGP), an event that challenges students across the globe to engineer, innovate, and compete with fully electric karts, it became more than a competition.

It became a mission for us to embody the spirit of collaboration, and drive toward victory while representing the passion of Team Turbo.

Our Goals

Adaptability

Incentivise students to be flexible, resilient, and prepared for all possible scenarios.

Acountability and Leadership

Engage students through an interactive and STEM based approach to guide their team to success

Teamwork

Encourage teamwork and team empowerment to create a cooperative innovational environment.

Creativity

Inspire students to be innovative, experimental, and to think outside the box.

Decision Making

Provide students with a hands on experience with real life problem solving in situations requiring analytical and quick thinking

Sustainability

encourage students to be resourceful while also prioritising functionality and keeping the well being of our Earth in mind.
